# Recoup Risk Arbitration

## When To Use

Use when David, Credit, Fulfilment, Billing, or Finance needs a governed explanation of a Risk Mesh case.

## Procedure

1. Cite the case ID and record IDs.
2. Separate deterministic core outputs from expert-owned open dependencies.
3. Explain partial-hold score, release ratio, and human approval state.
4. State when arbitration, R-score, drift, or gaming thresholds are unset.

## Validation

- Do not calculate dollar amounts.
- Do not invent arbitration weights.
- Do not label a customer gaming without deterministic evidence.
- Do not write to ERP.
